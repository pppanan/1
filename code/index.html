Pet-friendly Singapore

Nowadays, more and more people are choosing to keep pets, and the concept of pet-friendly cities has become a trending topic of discussion.Bringing pets along for short trips and hangouts has become a growing trend. To further enhance Singapore as a pet-friendly city, we aim to analyze pet-friendly restaurants and park facilities.

Through this interactive story map, we have sourced data from https://vanillapup.com/ and https://www.nparks.gov.sg/visit/parks/pets on the distribution of pet-friendly spots across Singapore. Our analysis focuses on their spatial distribution and walkability, assessing how accessible these locations are for pet owners and their furry companions.

In the crowdsourced map, We will collect newly discovered pet-friendly spots from users to refine the map and identify additional high-demand areas.
